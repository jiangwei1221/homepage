My name is Jiangwei Yu. I am a second-year Master's student in the Department of Computing Science, University of Alberta. I am working with Dr. Davood Rafiei in the Database Systems Group. Before coming to Canada, I got my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in China.

I am highly interested in text analytics and Web mining. Currently I am working on geotagging named entities in Web pages by modelling the relationship between locations and mentions of names in the context. Unsupervised algorithms can be directly applied to named entities in pages returned by a search engine. With the features generated by different models and prior-knowledge extracted from Wikipedia, we can further use Machine Learning techniques such as learning to rank to tackle this problem.

As expected, I will graduate in Summer 2014 and I am now looking for a job in the industry. You can find my relevant experience and my resume in this website.
